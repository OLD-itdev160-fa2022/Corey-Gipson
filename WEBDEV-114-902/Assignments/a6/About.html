ABOUT ME

Who Is The Tattoo Priest?

Meet Me:

Hello, my name is Shameir aka The Tattoo Priest, and I am dedicated to giving you the very best of tattoos with a very affordable price. My main focus is to give people an fun experience while getting tatted up!

In 2012 I started taking the art and craft of tattooing seriously, I started off practicing on my older brother until I got good enough to do other people tattoos. I have came a long ways since then, and after thousands and thousands of mess ups I finally was able to get good enough to launch my own full time business in 2019. I first started out, because of my passion for art. As a kid I always loved to draw. Instead of taking notes in class, I would be drawing in my notebook (which I dont recommend. Pay Attention In School Kids!) My love for art and God has drove me to not quit in the learning process of tattooing. I am now honored to be at a place now where I can do what I love full time.

I hope you enjoy the experience and the art of my tattoos as much as I enjoy offering them to you. The bible says “Each one should use whatever gift he has received to serve others, faithfully administering God’s grace in its various forms.” 1 Peter 4:10. I enjoy being of service to others with the gift God has giving me. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact me.

Sincerely,

The Tattoo Priest
