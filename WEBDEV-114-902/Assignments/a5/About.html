About Me

Welcome to Keep It Real Cuts, your number one source for the best haircuts in the city of Milwaukee. I am dedicated to giving you the very best experience while getting cut up by myself J-Tuck.

Founded in 2013, Keep It Real Cuts has come a long way from its beginnings in Dubuque, Iowa. When I first started out, my passion for listening to people and helping them out with sound advice has driven me to start cutting hair in college. Keep It Real Cuts can offer you the best cuts in the city as well with a place to chill and to enjoy yourself with the fellas. I am thrilled that I am is able to turn my passion into my own website.

I hope you enjoy your experience at Keep It Real Cuts as much as we enjoy offering our service to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact me.
